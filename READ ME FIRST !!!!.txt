FOOTBALL SCOREBOARD SYSTEM IN JAVASCRIPT


***Thanks To Anoopknr***

**Controls**

- A,S,D,F to change Goals
- J to start timer & resume
- K to pause
- R to reset Timer
- M,N Keys for Timer increment& decrement
